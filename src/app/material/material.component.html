August Life's album 'New Eternity', the brand new progressive melodic metal project initiated by Gert Nijboer and Bryan Ketelaars, saw the light of day late August 2018. The reactions of fans and press for this brand new outfit were overwhelming.

A bunch of great guest musicians and singers participated on NEW ETERNITY a.o. Peter Vink (Ayreon, KnightArea), Rob van der Loo (Epica, Mayan), Madicken de Vries (Heidevolk, Annatar) and the list goes on.

The CD was released worldwide through PT78 records and in 2019 August Life signed with the excellent label No Dust Records.


Musically, 'New Eternity' displays a perfect blend of a stylistic variety across the individual tracks while maintaining a unified cohesive lyrical theme.

Lyrically the album draws on the observations of Ketelaars on related events that can occur literally within seconds of each other.

'Coming Home' for instance is about a combatant heading home, 'Angels Of War' about high tech killing ISIS terrorists one by one and the title track is about brainwashed religionists.
